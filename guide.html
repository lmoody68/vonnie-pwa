💜 V.O.N.N.I.E. User Guide

👩‍⚕️ Caregivers & Families 🏥 Facility Admins 📋 Quick Reference

🚀 Getting Started

  1. 1
    Open the app

    Visit vonnie-aac-b2jrq.netlify.app on any phone, tablet, or computer. No download or account needed.

  2. 2
    Tap the welcome screen

    Vonnie will greet you with a warm spoken message. Tap anywhere on the opening screen to enter the app.

  3. 3
    Install it as an app (recommended)

    For instant one-tap access — especially important offline.

    📱 iPhone/iPad: Tap the Share button (⎙) → Add to Home Screen → Add
    🤖 Android: Tap the Install banner at the bottom of the screen → Install Now
    💻 Desktop: Click the install icon in your browser's address bar

🔊 Using the Communication Buttons

  1. 1
    Tap any button to speak

    The patient taps a colored button — Vonnie immediately speaks the phrase aloud. No typing required.

  2. 2
    Use REPEAT if needed

    Tap the purple 🔁 REPEAT button at the bottom to replay the last phrase.

  3. 3
    Stop speech anytime

    Tap the red ⏹ STOP button to immediately silence Vonnie.

  4. 4
    Say Anything (AI mode)

    Tap 💬 Say Anything to type any custom phrase. Vonnie's AI will shape it into natural speech and speak it aloud — useful for thoughts that aren't covered by the preset buttons.

  5. 5
    See more buttons

    Tap ➕ More Options to reveal a second grid of additional communication buttons.

🤝 Using Caregiver Mode

Caregiver Mode lets you ask structured YES/NO questions — perfect for patients with limited motor control who may find it easier to respond than to initiate.

  1. 1
    Switch to Caregiver Mode

    Tap the 🤝 Caregiver button in the top-right of the header. The screen transforms into a question board.

  2. 2
    Choose a category

    Select from: 🍽️ Basic Needs, 😌 Comfort, 💊 Pain & Medical, 💙 Feelings, 🎵 Activities, or ⭐ My Questions (your personalized questions).

  3. 3
    Tap a question

    Vonnie speaks the question aloud (e.g. "Are you in pain right now?") and a large YES/NO screen appears.

  4. 4
    Patient taps YES or NO

    Vonnie speaks the answer. The overlay closes automatically after 2 seconds — ready for the next question.

  5. 5
    Return to Patient Mode

    Tap 💙 Patient Mode in the header to restore the normal button grid.

🏥 Adding Custom Care Questions in Caregiver Mode

Create medical and care-specific YES/NO questions for your patient — tailored to their condition, medications, and clinical needs. Use starter templates or write your own.

  1. 1
    Switch to Caregiver Mode

    Tap 🤝 Caregiver in the top-right of the header.

  2. 2
    Tap the 🏥 My Care Questions tab

    This is the last tab in the category row, styled in blue. It shows all your personalized medical/care questions for this patient.

  3. 3
    Tap ➕ Add Care Question

    The medical question editor opens with three sections:

    💊 Medical Emoji Picker: Tap any medical icon (💊 🩺 🌡️ 🩹 🏥 🩸 💉 🧠 etc.) to select it for your question
    📋 Quick Templates: Tap any starter template to instantly fill the question field — then edit or save as-is (12 pre-written clinical questions provided)
    ❓ Question Text: Type or edit the question — must be answerable with YES or NO
  4. 4
    Tap ✅ Save Question

    The question appears instantly in your ⭐ My Questions tab.

  5. 5
    Ask the question

    Tap it — Vonnie speaks it aloud, the YES/NO screen appears, and the patient responds. Works exactly like the preset questions.

  6. 6
    Delete a question

    Hover over or long-press any custom question — a 🗑 delete button appears in the corner. Tap it to remove the question (you'll be asked to confirm).

💡 Good question examples: "Is your pain worse than earlier?", "Do you need your pain medication?", "Are you feeling nauseous?", "Is your IV site bothering you?", "Do you want me to call the nurse?" — medically relevant, always YES/NO answerable.

✏️ Adding & Editing Custom Buttons

  1. 1
    Open Settings

    Tap the ⚙️ gear icon in the top-right of the header.

  2. 2
    Scroll down and tap ✏️ Edit My Buttons

    This opens the button editor showing all your custom buttons.

  3. 3
    Add a new button

    Tap ➕ Add New Button. Then:

    😊 Pick an emoji from the grid (or type one in the box)
    📝 Enter a short Button Label (e.g. "My Show")
    🔊 Enter the full Phrase to Speak (e.g. "I want to watch my show please")
    🎨 Pick a color that helps the patient recognize this button
  4. 4
    Tap ✅ Save

    The button appears instantly in the ✨ My Buttons section of the app.

  5. 5
    Edit or delete existing buttons

    In the editor list, tap ✏️ to edit a button or 🗑️ to remove it.

📸 Important: After setting up buttons, take a screenshot of the editor list as a backup. If the browser is cleared, you'll know exactly what to recreate.

📞 Setting Up Family Phone Numbers

  1. 1
    Open Settings → Family Phone Numbers

    Enter phone numbers for Mom, NeNe, Grandma, or whoever the patient may want to reach.

  2. 2
    When the patient taps a Call button

    Vonnie will prompt to call the saved number. The caregiver or patient can confirm the call.

⚙️ Settings Overview

  1. 1
    Dark Mode

    Toggle dark mode for low-light environments (night shifts, dim rooms).

  2. 2
    Voice Speed

    Adjust how fast Vonnie speaks — slower is clearer, faster saves time.

  3. 3
    Voice Selection

    Choose from voices available on your device. Vonnie auto-selects the warmest available voice, but you can override it here. Tap 🎙️ Test Voice to preview.

  4. 4
    AI Integration

    Toggle individual AI providers on or off. Vonnie tries them in order — if one fails, it automatically moves to the next.

🏥 Creating Your Facility (First Time Setup)

  1. 1
    Open the Admin Dashboard

    Visit vonnie-aac-b2jrq.netlify.app/admin.html — share this URL only with authorized admins.

  2. 2
    Click "Create Facility"

    Switch to the Create Facility tab on the login screen.

  3. 3
    Fill in your facility details
    🏥 Facility Name: e.g. "Mercy General Hospital — ICU Ward"
    ⚕️ Facility Type: Hospital, Rehab, SNF, School, Home, or General
    📧 Admin Email: optional, for your records
    🔢 Admin PIN: choose a 4–6 digit PIN — you'll need this to sign in
    🔑 Registration Passcode: contact your V.O.N.N.I.E. administrator for this
  4. 4
    Save your facility code

    After creation, your unique Facility Code (e.g. MRC-4821) is displayed. Copy it and store it safely — caregivers need this code to link their tablets.

✅ Your facility is now registered. The Overview tab shows your facility code and a QR code caregivers can scan to join.

🎛️ Creating Button Sets

Button Sets are named collections of communication buttons you can push to any or all facility devices at once — no need to configure each tablet individually.

  1. 1
    Go to the Button Sets tab

    Click 🎛️ Button Sets in the dashboard navigation.

  2. 2
    Click ✨ New Button Set

    Give the set a descriptive name like "ICU Ward", "Stroke Recovery", or "Room 214 — John".

  3. 3
    Add buttons from the Preset Library

    Tap any preset button to add it to the set. You'll see a live preview of selected buttons below.

  4. 4
    Or import from a device

    Switch to the Import from Device tab. In the V.O.N.N.I.E. app on a tablet, go to Settings → export the button config as JSON, copy it, and paste it here.

  5. 5
    Save the Button Set

    Tap 💾 Save Button Set. The set appears in your dashboard.

  6. 6
    Set a Button Set as Active

    Tap Set Active on any button set. All linked devices will automatically load this set on their next app open.

📱 Registering Tablets / Devices

Two ways to link a tablet to your facility:

  1. 1
    Method A — QR Code (easiest)

    In the admin dashboard Overview tab, tap 🖨️ Print QR to open a printable QR code page. Print it and post it near each tablet station. The caregiver scans it with the tablet camera — the app opens and automatically prompts to join your facility.

  2. 2
    Method B — Manual code entry

    On the tablet, open V.O.N.N.I.E. → ⚙️ Settings → scroll to 🏥 Facility Mode → tap Join a Facility → enter the Facility Code (e.g. MRC-4821) and a label for that device (e.g. "Room 214 Tablet").

  3. 3
    Verify in the Devices tab

    Once joined, the tablet appears in the 📱 Devices tab with a green dot indicating it's active.

📡 Pushing Button Sets to All Devices

  1. 1
    Set a button set as Active

    In the Button Sets tab, tap Set Active on the configuration you want to push.

  2. 2
    Go to the Devices tab

    Tap 📡 Push to All Devices in the banner at the top.

  3. 3
    Devices update automatically

    Every linked tablet will load the new button set the next time the app is opened — no manual steps needed on each device.

  4. 4
    Assign different sets to specific devices

    In the Devices tab, use the dropdown next to any device to assign a specific button set — useful when different patients need different configurations.

🔑 Signing Back In

  1. 1
    Go to admin.html

    Visit vonnie-aac-b2jrq.netlify.app/admin.html

  2. 2
    Enter your Facility Code + PIN

    Use the Sign In tab. Enter the facility code (e.g. MRC-4821) and your 4–6 digit PIN.

⚠️ Lost your PIN? Contact your V.O.N.N.I.E. administrator — PIN resets require database access.

❓ Troubleshooting

  1. !
    Vonnie isn't speaking

    Make sure your device volume is turned up. On iOS, also check the silent/mute switch on the side. Try tapping Settings → 🎙️ Test Voice.

  2. !
    My custom buttons disappeared

    This happens if browser data was cleared. Open Settings → ✏️ Edit My Buttons and recreate them from your backup screenshot.

  3. !
    App showing old version

    Do a hard refresh: Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac). On mobile, close and reopen the app.

  4. !
    AI features not working

    AI requires an internet connection and API keys. Go to Settings → 🤖 AI Integration and make sure at least one provider has a valid key entered. The app still works fully offline without AI.

  5. !
    Facility buttons not updating on tablet

    The tablet syncs every 5 minutes. To sync immediately: Settings → 🏥 Facility Mode → 🔄 Sync Now.
