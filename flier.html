One-Tap Speech

Facility Admin NEW
Hospitals can push button sets to all tablets at once via QR code setup. No IT team required.

Works Offline

Cloud Backup NEW
Every custom button auto-saves to the cloud with a personal restore code. Lost your tablet? Restore in seconds.
